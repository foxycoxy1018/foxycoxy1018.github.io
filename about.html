    Instead, I am a person who spends a concerning amount of time watching The Great British Baking Show (which becomes stranger by the fact that I'm not even British) and is really bad at introductions.

    I am also a person who likes to write and likes to create. And because I have the soul of an 80-year old woman, I also often scrapbook so that I can preserve snapshots of who I was and what I was doing during different times of my life. You may think of this webpage as a space where I can do some combination of those three things.

    I hope you'll find something interesting or funny here. If you are interested in the coding and math stuff I've done, you may find those in the projects section (along with pictures of me baking weird Swedish cakes.)

    Oh, and my name is Carmen. Happy browsing!
