    Topological Data Analysis (TDA) on Art

    I "grew" images from their lightest to darkest pixels and kept track of the progression to find topological features. This project could be extended to include machine learning so images could be classified by a computer.

    TDA on Brain Arterial Trees

    During a summer REU, my group and I used persistent homology, statistics and machine learning on a dataset of brain arterial trees to analyze how brains change shape as they age.
